LapGenius

LapGenius is a smart e-commerce platform built for the Syrian market, focused on laptop sales. It features an AI-powered assistant that recommends devices based on users’ field of study and budget, as well as a tool that estimates fair market prices using local data.

Tabibona

Tabibona is a Remote Humanitarian Medical Platform We developed a platform that connects underserved patients with volunteer doctors to provide remote medical consultations at symbolic costs. The project aims to make healthcare more accessible for those unable to visit clinics.

Jobgate Syria

Jobgate Syria is a job-matching platform tailored for professionals and employers within Syria. Users can input their skills and explore relevant opportunities, making the job search easier, smarter, and more localized.
